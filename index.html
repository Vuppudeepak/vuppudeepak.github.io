Genetic Testing from Preserved Umbilical Cord

Deepak Vuppu co-authored a pioneering clinical study titled "Massive parallel sequencing of dried umbilical cord remnants", published in the American Journal of Medical Genetics. The study highlights the feasibility of using dried umbilical cord remnants for postmortem genetic testing in cases of suspected monogenic disorders. This method provides families with closure and future reproductive planning options when traditional DNA sources are unavailable.
