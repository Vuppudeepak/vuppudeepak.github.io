About

Dr. Deepak Vuppu is a purpose-driven healthcare leader with deep cross-functional experience across India, MEA, SEA, and global markets. With a strong clinical foundation (MBBS, MD Biochemistry) and executive business acumen from ISB’s PGPMAX, he has consistently delivered high-impact outcomes across diagnostics, oncology, and hospital verticals. Deepak has successfully led and scaled businesses with full P&L ownership exceeding ₹200 Cr, achieving over 35% CAGR over four consecutive years. His leadership blends strategic foresight with operational excellence, grounded in Lean Six Sigma Green and Black Belt certifications. He brings a global perspective through international immersion experiences at the London Business School and The Wharton School, and is passionate about leveraging technology and AI to transform access, efficiency, and quality of care in healthcare ecosystems.

Core Competencies

  • Institutional and Government Healthcare Strategy
  • Digital Health, Diagnostics, and Biotech Commercialization
  • Leadership Across Multicultural and International Markets
